Project Location


This map highlights the key features of Summit Air’s carbon removal project in Wyoming.

  • Project Site: Central hub for Direct Air Capture operations
  • CO₂ Injection Well: Secure underground storage location
  • Pipeline Pressure Sensor: Real-time monitoring system
  • Gas Power Plant: Provides baseload energy to support DAC operations
  • Wind Energy Area: Contributes renewable energy to reduce emissions

The layout is designed to leverage Wyoming’s strengths in energy infrastructure and geology, while supporting long-term climate goals and regional development.
