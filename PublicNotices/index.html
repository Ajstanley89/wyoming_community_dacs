Public Notices

Summit Air is committed to transparency and timely communication with Wyoming communities. This section provides access to official notices, regulatory filings, hearing announcements, and opportunities for public comment—ensuring that stakeholders stay informed and engaged throughout the project lifecycle.

What You'll Find Here

  • Permitting Notices: Updates on state and federal permits, including Class VI injection wells and environmental reviews.
  • Hearing Announcements: Scheduled public hearings, town halls, or informational meetings regarding project milestones.
  • Regulatory Filings: Submissions to the Wyoming DEQ, EPA, and other relevant agencies made accessible for public review.
  • Comment Opportunities: Notices of when and how local residents, landowners, and tribal members can submit input on development plans.
  • Archived Communications: A record of previous notices and public engagement activities for transparency and accountability.

Public involvement is essential to responsible energy development. Whether you're a local resident, business owner, tribal member, or community leader, we encourage you to explore the resources here and participate in the ongoing dialogue around this project.

Community Engagement & Meeting Requests

Summit Air is committed to transparent, respectful, and inclusive dialogue with Wyoming communities, including local governments, landowners, and the Northern Arapaho Tribe. This section allows stakeholders to request meetings with our project team, ask for formal notices, or schedule briefings tailored to their needs.

How We Collaborate

  • Community Briefings: Informational sessions for local groups, councils, or organizations to learn about project updates, safety, and opportunities.
  • Tribal Consultation: Ongoing availability to meet with tribal leaders and representatives in accordance with sovereignty and cultural respect.
  • Event Participation: Request a Summit Air speaker or exhibit at community events, school programs, or workforce fairs.
  • Public Notice Requests: Submit a formal request to receive advance notification about permit filings, hearing dates, or milestones affecting your area.
